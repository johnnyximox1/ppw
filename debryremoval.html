Debris

What is Debris

=> Debris are the scattered remains of something broken or destroyed. Actually it depends on Investor’s decision. Whatever the things if Investor want to dump that is Debris.

We can categorize Debris into 3 types –

  1. General Debris
  2. Personals
  3. Hazards

General Debris

We can divided into 2 category –

  1. Interior Debris : Located within the Main Building/Garages/Attics/Basements/Sheds/Crawl Spaces
  2. Exterior Debris: Located outside of the Main Building (Yard/Porch/Deck)

Personal property

=> Personal property is generally considered the property that is movable. Whatever the thing if Investor wants to store it then we considered the property as Personals.

Hazard is a situation that poses a level of threat to Life, Health or Environment.  Most hazards are dormant or potential, with a theoretical risk of harm. We divided Hazards into 4 category –

  1. Environmental Hazards : Materials that can cause environmental hazards as Dead Animal remaining in the open space of the property.
  2. Health Hazards : Materials that can cause Health Hazards as Raw Foods remaining in the open space of the property
  3. Trip Hazards : Materials that can cause Trip Hazards as Brick/Exposed Wire remaining in the ground of the property.
  4. Safety Hazards : Materials that can cause human risk by touching unintentionally as Exposed or loose wire/Hole in a deck/Missing Handrail in stairs/Missing Railing in porch/Insecure pool.
