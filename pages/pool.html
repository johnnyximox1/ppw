Pool Maintenance

Draining pool

Role of Chlorine in water- As a halogen, chlorine is a highly efficient disinfectant, and is added to public water supplies to kill disease-causing pathogens, such as bacteria, viruses, and protozoans, that commonly grow in water supply reservoirs, on the walls of water mains and in storage tanks. If there is imbalance of chlorine in the pool water, that time we need to drain the pool along treat and shock before refilling with new water.

Process of Draining

  • Few chemicals and materials like pump, hose pipe, generator (if electricity found off) and other items needed to drain a pool.
  • Connecting hose pipes with pump and other part into the jungle or the place where the water can be flown of. And the sump pump will be set under the water for a proper pumping.
  • Start draining after all the equipment's being set properly.
  • Applying pressure wash to remove moss from the pool interior.
  • After draining old and dirty pool water, Re-check or change the pool filter to keep the water clean by changing the water schedule wise.
  • Applying chlorine to keep the water clear.


After completing treating, cleaning, and draining. Pool filled up with new water. Chemicals are mixed along with the water to be in real color.
